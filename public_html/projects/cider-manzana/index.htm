Manzana is an apple, but only in Spanish. Spain, as an open-air museum, will always accompany the one who purchases this drink. Incredible country, incredible sound. The word seems to be filled with the sun and energy, emotionally binds to itself, and just asks: "Come back to me, I will give you the sun."

Due to the fact that the name is sunny, bright, and Spanish, we transferred this mood emotionally to the design of the bottle. It turned out to be a bright, juicy, and memorable concept that not only pleases consumers but also makes them want to buy and refresh themselves with a new drink.
